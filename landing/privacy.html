Privacy Policy

Last updated: 18 July 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how DREVORA handles information in connection with the marketing website at drevora.app and the fleet and workforce management web application at app.drevora.app (together, the “Services”).

1. Who we are

DREVORA is a fleet and workforce management software platform for transport and related businesses. For privacy questions, contact admin@drevora.uk.

2. Information we collect

Depending on how you interact with DREVORA, we may process:

  • Demo and contact information submitted through the landing-page demo request form, such as full name, company name, work email address, approximate vehicle count, and an optional message.
  • Account and authentication information used to access the web application, such as login credentials and account identifiers associated with authorised users.
  • Customer operational data that organisations choose to enter into the platform in the course of using DREVORA (for example fleet, worker, timesheet, holiday, vehicle-check, document, and related operational records).
  • Technical information ordinarily generated when using a hosted web service, such as browser type, device information, and basic request logs needed to operate and secure the Services.

We do not intentionally collect special categories of personal data through the marketing website demo form.

3. How we use information

We use information to:

  • respond to demo requests and product enquiries;
  • provide, maintain, and support the DREVORA platform for customer organisations;
  • authenticate users and protect accounts and systems;
  • communicate about service-related matters;
  • improve reliability, usability, and security of the Services;
  • comply with legal obligations that apply to us.

We do not sell personal data. We do not use the landing website for advertising profiling or third-party advertising analytics.

4. Legal bases

Where data protection law requires a legal basis, we typically rely on one or more of the following, depending on the context: performance of a contract or steps prior to entering a contract; legitimate interests in operating and securing a B2B software service; consent where required; and compliance with legal obligations.

5. Service providers and hosting

To operate the Services we use trusted infrastructure and service providers, including:

  • Vercel — hosting for the marketing website and related serverless API endpoints used by the landing page.
  • Resend — email delivery for demo requests submitted through the landing-page form (messages are sent to our operations inbox).
  • Cloud application infrastructure used to host the authenticated DREVORA web application, authentication, and customer operational data storage.

These providers process information on our instructions and only as needed to deliver their services. We do not disclose personal data for unrelated third-party marketing.

6. Cookies and similar technologies

The DREVORA marketing website is a static product site. Based on the current landing implementation, it does not load third-party advertising, analytics, or tracking pixels.

The authenticated web application may use cookies or similar browser storage that are necessary for session management, authentication, and core product functionality. Those technologies are used to operate the product, not for advertising profiling on the marketing website.

A detailed list of verified browser storage technologies is available in our Cookie Policy. You can also control cookies and site storage through your browser settings. Blocking essential authentication storage may prevent login or other core functions of the web application from working correctly.

7. Sharing of information

We may share information:

  • with service providers who help us host, deliver email, or operate the Services;
  • with members of a customer organisation according to that organisation’s access controls;
  • when required by law, regulation, or valid legal process;
  • to protect the rights, security, and integrity of DREVORA, our users, or others;
  • in connection with a business transfer, where permitted and with appropriate safeguards.

8. Retention

We retain information for as long as needed to provide the Services, respond to enquiries, meet legal or accounting requirements, and resolve disputes. Demo request emails and related correspondence are retained for a reasonable business period. Customer organisations control much of the operational data they store in the platform during an active subscription.

9. Security

We apply technical and organisational measures appropriate to the nature of a hosted business software service. No method of transmission or storage is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security of information.

10. International transfers

Our service providers may process information in the United Kingdom, European Economic Area, or other countries where they operate. Where required, we rely on appropriate transfer mechanisms made available by those providers.

11. Your rights

Depending on applicable law, you may have rights to access, correct, delete, or restrict processing of personal data, to object to certain processing, or to lodge a complaint with a supervisory authority. To exercise a request related to DREVORA, email admin@drevora.uk.

If you are a worker or user of a customer organisation’s DREVORA workspace, some requests may need to be handled by that organisation as the party controlling the operational records.

12. Children

The Services are directed to businesses and adult professionals. They are not intended for children.

13. Changes to this Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The “Last updated” date at the top of this page will change when updates are published. Material changes will be communicated in a manner appropriate to the context, such as by updating this page.

14. Contact

Privacy questions: admin@drevora.uk.

Related terms of use are available in our Terms of Service. Browser cookies and similar technologies are described in our Cookie Policy.
